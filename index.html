About

Pharmacist and data scientist working at the intersection of clinical pharmacy, machine learning, and health systems in Africa.

Currently serving as Finance and Operations Manager at KAM AID Pharmacy Ltd., overseeing multi-branch operations across Greater Accra while developing data infrastructure that transforms dispensing and claims data into actionable clinical and operational insights.

Founder of Aduru Analytics, an AI-focused initiative building pharmaceutical intelligence systems for inventory optimization, anomaly detection, and medication adherence prediction in resource-constrained settings.

Research interests include medication adherence, pharmaceutical outcomes, health equity, and non-communicable disease burden — with a focus on applying machine learning to real-world healthcare data in African populations. My work emphasizes translating data-driven research into scalable, context-aware health systems solutions.

Currently pursuing PhD opportunities in Health Informatics, Pharmaceutical Outcomes, and Public Health Data Science — with screening calls confirmed at the University of Pittsburgh and University of Michigan.
